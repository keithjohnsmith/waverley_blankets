    About us

    Waverley Blankets is a proud Zimbabwean company that is committed to providing its customers with the highest quality blankets. The company's blankets are a testament to the skill and craftsmanship of Zimbabwean workers, and they are a source of warmth and comfort for millions of people around the world.
